<div class ="design">
        <div class="Process">
            Design Process
        </div>
        <ul class="list">
            <li>Problem Statement and Research
                <ul class="points">
                    <li>Clearly define the problem and conduct user research to understand the users needs, 
                        motivations, and behaviours
                    </li>
                    <li>Study competitors and trends in the current market</li>
                    <li>Create user personas representing the primary user groups</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li>Project Goals and Planning
                <ul class="points">
                    <li>Map out user journeys and use cases to understand how the user interacts with the product</li>
                    <li>Define key design goals based on the problem statement, user needs, and business goals</li>
                    <li>Plan the structure of the information to ensure its logical and intuitive</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li>Mockups and Design
                <ul class="points">
                    <li>High level sketches and wireframes to outline basic design elements</li>
                    <li>Develop low level prototypes that include all interactive components, this should be close
                        to the final design and consider design elements such as typography, colours, and imagery
                    </li>
                    <li>Ensure considerations such as accessibility are taken into account in the final design</li>

                </ul>
            </li>
            <li>User Testing and Feedback
                <ul class="points">
                    <li>Craft a survey that considers ..... potential downfalls</li>
                    <li>Gather feedback from users on usibility and overall experience</li>
                    <li>Based on the feedback from the user testing phase make necessary changes</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li>Documentation and Design Handoff
                <ul class="points">
                  <li>Provide detailed design documents including layouts, graphics, and interactive elements</li>
                  <li>Colloborate closely with developers and particpate in final testing to ensure quality ensurence</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li>Next Steps
                <ul class="points">
                    <li>Deploy and monitor the product monitoring user interactions</li>
                    <li>Gather post launch feedback and implement that into continuous iterative improvment
                        to ensure the product continues to meet evolving user needs
                    </li>
                </ul>
            </li>
        </ul>
    </div>
